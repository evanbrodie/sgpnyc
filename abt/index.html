Flagship Applications

Document Compliance System

DOX is designed to efficiently generate, track and maintain all document, covenant and tickler requirements. The system mitigates risk and provides confidence to auditors and rating agencies through automation.

Real Estate Portfolio System

REDS oversees the running of real estate loan portfolios. Deals are tracked from approval through expiration. The window-based operating memorandums, escrow administration and on-demand reporting allow for oversight and control.

Data Warehouse Manager

Centralized report management with role-based access control, automated scheduling, and Power BI integration for dynamic dashboards and reporting.

Process Management

Customizable workflows with 4-eye approvals, real-time tracking, and intuitive reporting to streamline and automate your business processes.
