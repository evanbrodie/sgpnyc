Real Estate Deals System

Collateral Management for Real Estate Loans
🏢 ManageManage and control the surveillance process in an adaptable framework
📂 ProvideProvide a central repository for deal terms and property metrics
📈 AggregateAggregate key monitoring statistics and automate reporting

The Solution

REDS oversees the internal management of commercial real estate loan portfolios through client-driven system feeds. Deals are tracked from pipeline through expiration, using version-based operating memorandums and workflow ticklers.

Additional functionality for portfolio administration, servicing, and reporting provide comprehensive oversight and control throughout the entire deal cycle.

Completed framework adaptable in multiple environments.

Key Benefits

REDS has an intuitive layout for easy input of deal data.

Flexibility within REDS allows the user to customize reporting and perform ad hoc analysis.

Once data is entered, it is stored and cannot be altered or deleted in error. A full audit log provides additional support.

Main Functions

  • Operating Memorandums — Completed at closing, restructure, amendment and extension, and chronicle the full deal history.
  • Syndication Functionality — Participant bank data, skim margin and fees and Shared National Credit reporting.
  • Collateral Monitoring — Hedge tracking, collateral and leasing Letters of Credit, lockbox details and escrow accounts.
  • Ongoing Metrics — NOI, revenues, occupancy can be used for rollups to measure portfolio performance.
